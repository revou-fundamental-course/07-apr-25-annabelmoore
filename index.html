Our Profile

Annabel Moore

Founder

"Get Active" began when founder Annabel Moore noticed a growing disconnect between city life and well-being. A lifelong advocate for activity and healthy eating, she started informal hikes and recipe sharing, seeing the positive impact and realizing the need for accessible guidance. This inspired her to create "Get Active," aiming for a holistic, supportive community to make healthy living sustainable and enjoyable, beyond just workouts or diets.

Fueled by this passion and early success, Annabel founded "Get Active" with her own resources and community encouragement. The name reflects her core belief: empowering everyone to actively pursue their well-being. From these small beginnings, driven by a desire to make a difference, "Get Active" has become a dedicated team helping people unlock their potential through movement, nourishment, and a positive mindset.
